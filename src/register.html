FAQ

To register for the races please follow the steps below:

  1. ACA waiver - Please download, complete and bring it with you to the races. You must have a signed waiver in order to race.
    • Adult ACA Waiver
    • Minor ACA Waiver
  2. Follow the link below to register:
    • You are about to be taken to Paddle Guru to register. It is advantageous for you to create an account, as we will be using Paddle Guru for the foreseeable future and registering will be easier going forward.
    • Once you fill out the registration, you will have the choice to pay online or pay the day of the race. (Paying day of race fees will be higher than online rates.)
    • If you are uncomfortable paying online, please contact us directly. president@alabamacupraces.com.
    • Paddle Guru
