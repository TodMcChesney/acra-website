First, we would like to thank all the volunteers for their help in the past!

How can you help out?

There are many ways that you can help out. These jobs may require climbing up and down rocky banks of the river, but if you have a condition that doesn't allow for that, we can find something that doesn't require as much climbing.

There are two shifts per race day that lasts about 4 hours each. You can volunteer for only one or both shifts. Volunteers should arrive no later than 8 am for morning shift and no later than 11:45 am for afternoon shifts.

Our main need is for Gate Judges

If you've never judged before, it is really easy. Download the Gate Judging 101 image and read over it. An example of a race score sheet can be found here.

If you have never judged before, it is recommended you review this information before coming for the races. It is also recommended, but not required, to come to our training day before the race season begins. Once a date has been determined, that date will be posted on our Facebook page and will also be emailed to our volunteers.

Be sure to come prepared!

It is recommended that you bring a bag (such as a backpack) with things you may need or want. Some suggested items are:

  • Hat
  • Sun glasses
  • Sun block
  • Layered clothing (It's usually cool in the mornings and warm during the day)
  • Poncho (if rain is possible)

Snacks and water will be supplied for volunteers and additional food will be available for purchase. FREE “race crew” T-SHIRT for all volunteers working two shifts or more. Event fee is free, but camping fees do apply for all volunteers.

Volunteers needed for:

  • One volunteer needed for list
  • Set up gates and site preparations (work weekends always scheduled the weekend before each race, unless water levels prohibit) and the Friday of race weekend for course set up
  • Set up tents/tables/chairs etc... (the day before each race)
  • Registration
  • Entrance tent
  • Parking control
  • Cooks in charge of grills
  • People to assist the cooks
  • Sales of food and merchandise
  • Food server for awards dinner
  • Safety boaters
  • Starters
  • Timing - Scoring
  • Gate judges
  • PA announcers
  • Scoring table
  • First Aid tent
